PRODUCT NAME
============
WeddingAI


ONE-SENTENCE PITCH
==================
WeddingAI helps engaged couples see their actual wedding venue styled as their
wedding day by using Gemini to design a theme from a phone video of the empty
room and render the room restyled in it, replacing weeks of imagination and
thousands in visualisation fees with about a minute.


SHORTER VARIANT (if a character limit bites)
============================================
WeddingAI turns a phone video of an empty venue into a Gemini-designed wedding
theme and a photorealistic render of that exact room styled for the day.


TAGLINE
=======
Walk into your wedding before it exists.
